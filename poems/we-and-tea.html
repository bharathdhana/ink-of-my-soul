We and Tea

On a cold evening, with rainfall and a chill breeze,
The skies are dusky, and raindrops nourish the warmth.

We are wrapped under the blanket
Cuddling close,
Whispering softly to one another,
Our song played in the background.
With cups of tea in our hands,
The chill draws us nearer-
Yet holding you feels like the truest warmth.

Shadows flicker under the glow of the flame.
You and I,
Lost in the safety of each other's loving arms.
