Goodbye and Goodbye

Denial—
Empty room where time stood still,
I craved your smile on every frame.
Spoke your name like song or mantra—
As if pretending could prove me wrong.
Anger—
Rage lit up the empty space,
Everything around me sounded loud—
The sky, the earth, the voices.
I cursed your fate for leaving me
To bear the weight.
All this anger was once love.
Bargaining—
I starred at the night sky,
Whispered to the midnight-bright stars
“Just bring them back, undo these scars”
Wished upon the universe—
For it to bend,
For chance to mend.
Depression—
Silence all around the walls
Heaviness stole my rest.
Already dead, keeping myself alive,
To decorate the coffin
With love and lies.
Acceptance—
Yet slowly, light began to spill,
Through cracks, I once had meant to fill.
I speak your name with gentle grace—
Love lingers, though I miss your face…
The last words for you:
Goodbye, and
Goodbye.
