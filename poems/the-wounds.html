The Wounds

Immense pain—
Caused by yourself, or someone else.
Immeasurable, Unbearable.
Wounds are gifts,
Given by those we love.
Cut. Sharp. Deeper.
They say—
Time heals everything.
Never.
Time only teaches you how to live with it.
Even wounds are traumatised.
Numb to pain, even when bleeding.
Yet clingy.
Bandages, dressings—they won't fix it.
Don't touch the wound
In the middle of healing.
Let it be.
One touch—nostalgia.
It may scare you,
It may ache.
Yet,
Never touch it.
Leave it as it was.
In the end—
Wounds may become scars.
Scars with memories.
