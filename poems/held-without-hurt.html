Held Without Hurt

At the spark of something,
You’ve been showing me.
A feeling I’ve never known in my entire life.
No wonder—
Why are you doing this to me?
Why me, when you could find someone better?
I’ve been pushing you away,
Yet you keep showing up.
Again and again.
Why?
What do you see in me?
What in me caught your eyes?
I like the way you say my name.
There’s so much I want to share—
But maybe it’s better kept quiet.
Still, I’m grateful.
You’re the one holding me,
Without ever hurting me.

You’ve become the thought I can’t ignore—
A comfort I never knew I was craving.
I’ve built walls no one was meant to climb,
But you’re not breaking them…
You’re just standing there, patient.
And maybe that means something.
It scares me—
Because this doesn’t feel like a game.
It feels like someone might actually stay.
And maybe, for the first time,
I don’t want to run.

If your heart speak honesty,
And your touch holds no harm…
Then maybe—
I’ll let you:
Touch me,
Hold me,
Kiss me—
Like you see all of me.
