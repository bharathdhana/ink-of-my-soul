Isn't it Enough?

I have crossing miles for you—
Didn’t you see the way I long for you?
A touch of longevity from you,

This heart, once yours,
Can hold the pain,
But can never love again—
That is the only gift I have left to offer.

Didn’t you see my wounds?
Bleeding for you,
Marked by the scars you give.

Don’t take it for granted—
This Love, this soul I surrendered,
Was meant to bring peace and harmony.

Why can’t you see?
The love, the care I held for you.
An admirer, always watching—
Like gazing at the moon in a lonely night sky.

Wondering, what went wrong?
Me? You? Us?
Was I not enough?
Was love not enough?
Was it ever enough?
Isn’t it enough?
