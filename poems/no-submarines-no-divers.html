No Submarines, No Divers

Rustling mind, fogged out
I wish I could pack my emotions,
Fold my feelings into a black suitcase—
Toss them into the deepest ocean,
Where no one could ever reach.
No submarines,
No divers.
No depth could find that grief.
Storms and thunders front and back—
I'm running down an endless highway
Trying to save myself… from me.
No signboards,
No cars,
Just grey, angry clouds
Trying to exorcise my soul.
They fall from the sky—
No planes.
No birds.
Just screaming,
Louder…
Until my voice dies.
Striking the ground
At the speed of despair—
Bang!
Now I'm levitating in space,
Crushed by asteroids.
Looking in the mirror…
Love.
A Glitch.
Sore eyes.
Tre-- Trembling hands.
Cold heart.
Scars etched deep into my soul.
Mirrors shattered.
And me…
Shattered too.
