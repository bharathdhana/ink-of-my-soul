Beauty in Nothing

I'm nothing, talking to you,
I'm nothing.
Find me in your peace and solace.

Rooms filled with echoes of voices,
Yet I am filled with emptiness.
But emptiness isn't a sin—
It's seeking yourself, your soul, in peace.

Zero thoughts, zero emotions,
A blank page, left behind,
Chose to leave it unwritten.

No conclusions, no endings—
Well, nothing has nothing to say.
Surrender. Free your soul.
You will find
Beauty in nothing.
