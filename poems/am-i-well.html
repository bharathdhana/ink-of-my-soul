Am I Well?

I don’t feel well.
My mind is screaming
Voices in my head—haunting me,
A full recap of all I did,
And all I didn’t.

Career, friends, and the connections I’ve made—
Now questioning me,
Betrayed.
Cornered and crooked in the dimming light.
Love and relationships—the worst part.
Taken for granted, trash cans.
The efforts I made?
Just a topic in her friends’ gossips.

Wondering…
Why was I made this way?
Questioning my very existence—why all of this?
The room is filled with haunting shadows of the past,
My soul screams beneath an ocean of darkness.
Blinded. Rustled. Bruised.
Running for so long through this dark room,
And no shining door to escape through.

A sad, sad symphony plays,
Unraveling a city of lost hopes
And unanswered prayers.
I can’t see anything in there.
Just hopeless, slow music in my mind,
Interrupted by
Voiceless cries…
I can’t feel anything—
Not even me.
