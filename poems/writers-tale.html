Writer's Tale

O Beloved,
O Beloved,
A writer's love
Is different.

If a writer loves you,
You will never die —
Their words will melt
You down to the core.

If the writer wrote Come,
She came.
If the writer wrote Sit,
She sat.

If he wrote Lay your head on my shoulder,
She rested there.
He wrote Sleep,
And she drifted away.

If he wrote Lost,
She wandered,
And when he wrote Restless,
She became uneasy.
Seeing her pain, he stumbled — heartache.
So he removed less from the word,
So she could rest.

When the writer wrote Special,
She felt butterflies stir within her.
When he wrote Aim,
She chased after him.
When he wrote Love,
She fell for him.
And when he wrote Stay,
She stayed — forever.
